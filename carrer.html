Product Design Expert

Share
Requirement: User Interface and User Experience Designer
Position: Product Designer
Experience: 3 years plus exclusively in Figma and AdobeXD. Over all experience 6 years plus.
Location: Goa or Mumbai (Also open for flexible remote working)
CTC: As per industry standards

Responsibilities of Product Designer
  • Plan and implement new designs 
  • Optimize existing user interface designs
  • Test for intuitivity and experience
  • Communicate with Business/Team Heads to understand their business goals and objectives
  • Develop technical and business requirements and deliver intuitive and user-centered solutions
  • Combine creativity with an awareness of the design elements
  • Create prototypes for new product ideas
  • Test new ideas before implementing 
  • Conduct an ongoing user research

Skills and Qualifications
  • Overall 6 plus years of experience as a Creative Designer/visualizer
  • A minimum of 3 years plus of experience as a UI/UX designer
  • A minimum of 2 years plus experience in a Creative Design Studio or Advertising Agency
  • A minimum of 1 year plus of experience with HTML5 & CSS3 (not mandatory, but added advantage)
  • A must and excellent working knowledge of:
  • Figma or Adobe XD
  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator (basics)
  • PowerPoint / Slides / Keynote
  • Excellent  interpersonal and communication skills
  • Should be a:
  • Critical Thinker
  • Problem Solver
  • Detailed Oriented
